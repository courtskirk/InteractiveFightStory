You Chose to Move Around

You start backing up, but you trip over your own feet and fall on your back. 

			"God, I don't want to fight this girl", you think to yourself as she gets closer.

You begin to try and stand, and she sets her legs to throw a kick.

You automatically think to block it, that was until you felt a sudden wave of heat, and notice a flame igniting at the bottom of her bare foot.

Your eyes widen in fear...
